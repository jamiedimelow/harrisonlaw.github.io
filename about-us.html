About us

  • Harrison Legal Limited was established on the 29th July 2019 by Caroline Harrison.

    After working for over twelve years in Glasgow and then Kilmarnock as a Solicitor, Caroline felt that it was time to establish her own practice specialising in family and child law. She has a passion for representing her clients at Children’s Hearings and all associated proceedings to the best of her ability. She is not shy to challenge the intervention of the state in family life and to ensure that all of her clients receive fair and independent treatment at these hearings whilst dealing with some of the most difficult times in their families lives.

    Caroline has vast experience in raising and defending court proceedings in Sheriff Courts and in the Court of Session and whatever the matter is that you require advice on she will endeavour to obtain the best result possible.

    Caroline understands how important her clients cases are to them and deals with cases efficiently and without delay. She has ensured that her firm is not just a normal business hours firm and provides a 24 hour emergency telephone line to ensure that if you are facing an emergency situation and require legal advice then she is on hand to provide you with the necessary guidance and support. She appreciates that life does not come to a halt at 5pm!

    Also working in the firm is Lindsay Dempsey, Office Manager. Lindsay has been Caroline’s right hand woman since her move to Kilmarnock and has excellent people skills and knows all clients cases and is able to speak to clients and answers the majority of queries whilst Caroline is at court.

    Chris is our legal assistant who is in regular contact with the clients updating them in respect of their cases and taking statements from witnesses. If you wish to contact him then please do so on chris@harrisonlaw.legal or 07984 331518.

    We carry out legal aid work and private work. We can carry out a financial calculation over the telephone with you to establish your eligibility prior to your appointment.
